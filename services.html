PEB Construction

Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB) represent the future of fast, cost-effective, high-quality construction. Ikon Constructions' PEB systems are fabricated off-site to precision specifications, then assembled on-location — slashing construction time and cost without sacrificing quality.

With lightweight steel structures and modern cladding solutions, our PEB projects meet international standards for structural safety, energy efficiency, and long-term durability.

How We Work

Our Construction Process

Every Ikon project follows a proven 6-step process that ensures quality, transparency, and on-time delivery — from your first call to key handover.

💬
Step 01

Initial Consultation

We listen to your vision, understand your requirements, budget, and site conditions in a detailed free consultation.

📐
Step 02

Design & Planning

Our architects create detailed blueprints, 3D renders, structural designs, and a transparent cost estimate.

📋
Step 03

Approvals & Agreement

We handle all government approvals, RERA registration, legal documentation, and sign a transparent contract.

🏗️
Step 04

Construction Phase

Skilled teams execute each phase with quality-controlled materials, regular site inspections, and client updates.

🔍
Step 05

Quality Inspection

Independent structural audits, snag list reviews, and final quality checks before any handover.

🗝️
Step 06

Key Handover

A proud moment — we hand you the keys to your dream space with all documentation and warranty in place.
