About The Project

A New Standard of Premium Living

Silver Heights is Ikon Constructions' most prestigious residential development — a thoughtfully designed apartment complex in the heart of Guduvancheri, one of Chennai's fastest-growing residential corridors.

With only 8 exclusive units across 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4 BHK configurations, Silver Heights offers an intimate, premium living experience that larger developments simply cannot provide. Every apartment is a masterpiece of space utilisation, natural lighting, and modern design.

From smart home automation to solar water heating, EV charging to 24/7 security — every amenity has been carefully curated to elevate your daily life.
