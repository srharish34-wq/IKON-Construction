Who We Are

More Than a Builder — We Are Your Partner

Ikon Constructions is a leading architectural and construction firm headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Founded in 2020, we have rapidly grown into one of the most trusted names in residential and commercial construction across the city.

We excel in creating innovative, unique designs with bold execution — tailoring every solution to the diverse needs of our clients. Our expertise spans interior design, exterior architecture, and full structural construction — blending creativity with functionality to bring your vision to life.

What sets us apart is our holistic approach: we don't just build structures, we craft lasting relationships and spaces that tell your unique story.

Foundation

Ikon Constructions Founded

Mohamed Ishaq Syed and R. Karaunainathan establish Ikon Constructions in Chennai with a bold vision — to redefine premium residential construction in Tamil Nadu.

2021
First Milestone

First 5 Projects Delivered

Successfully completed our first five residential projects across Tirumalisai, MM Nagar, and Maraimalai Nagar — earning a 5-star rating from every client.

2022
Growth

Expanded to Commercial Projects

Moved into commercial construction and PEB (Pre-Engineered Buildings) — bringing our quality-first approach to the corporate sector with multiple projects across Chennai.

2023
Innovation

Smart Home Integration Launched

Pioneered smart home integration across all new projects — incorporating IoT systems, solar water heating, EV charging stations, and automated security into every build.

2024
